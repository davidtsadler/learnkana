Learn Kana

Welcome to the learn kana webapp. A memory aid to help in your learning of hiragana and katakana.

Played over a number of rounds you are shown at random a romanised kana character. The aim is to choose the correct hiragana or katakana from the four answers. For each correct answer you will score 3 points but you will lose 1 point for each wrong answer.
